About

8 Keys Studio began when our group of friends, who love playing video games, decided to try making some. Somehow, even though it probably should have been a bad idea to just jump into something without any real training, it ended up being really fun. We started out by participating in game jams and worked on developing ideas in our spare time. We have made five games this way, and are working on our first complete project. We come from a range of backgrounds and professions, but we all like the internet and coffee and cats. We never have any trouble finding a place to order together from on Seamless. We are based primarily in Brooklyn and New Jersey.
